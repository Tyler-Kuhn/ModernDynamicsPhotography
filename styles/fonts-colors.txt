
Fonts:

Playfair Display:
Usage: This serif font has high contrast and distinctive style, making it suitable for headlines and titles.
CSS: font-family: 'Playfair Display', serif;

Josefin Sans:
Usage: This sans-serif is sleek and geometric, which can complement the serif headline font for subtitles and 
secondary text.
CSS: font-family: 'Josefin Sans', sans-serif;

Lora:
Usage: This serif font is well-suited for body text due to its readability and classic style, providing a 
good balance with the more decorative fonts used for headings.
CSS: font-family: 'Lora', serif;
